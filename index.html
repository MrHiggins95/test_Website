Journey to the West

Journey to the West is a Chinese novel published in the 16th century during the Ming dynasty. It follows the Buddhist monk Xuanzang as he journeys to India to collect Buddhist scriptures, accompanied by the mythical Monkey King, Sun Wukong. The novel is a blend of mythology, philosophy, and adventure, reflecting enduring Chinese cultural attitudes and beliefs.
